The Bodega Incident

Oh hi! Thanks for trying out this new egamebook thing. This is a very early preview. I'm sorry to say the story is more of a draft, and it's still not playable start-to-finish.

If things break (and they will), try refreshing the website in your browser and/or click the Restart button. Also, the interface can be slow or laggy at times. I'm really working hard on fixing all this. I would love to hear your feedback. Please send me an email with your comments, critique or bug reports. Thanks!!

You are the last living person on the Bodega, a large cargo ship stranded in the middle of an interstellar flight. You are alone and dying. The only person who seemed to know what he was doing just passed away. The hyperdrive doesn't work. The ship hates you. And things are only going to get worse.

This book was written by Filip Hracek and illustrated by Jan Dolezalek.
