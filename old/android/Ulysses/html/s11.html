The young ensign pressed some buttons, then said, "Hailing the Sunriser. Channel open, Captain."

Captain Donaldson stood and addressed the main display screen. His image and words were transmitted to the other starship as he spoke, "This is Captain Donaldson, of the F.S.S. Ulysses. I greet you as a fellow member of the Federation of Worlds."

A view of the Bridge of the Sunriser appeared on the main display screen, zooming in on the tiger-like captain of the military cruiser. Like those aboard the Ulysses, the captain wore the navy-blue and gold trim of a Federation Space Services officer.

"I greet you in return, Captain Donaldson. I am named Calimbaer, captain of the F.S.S. Sunriser. I request you explain your presence at Pegasi station," the Cassian captain said, making it sound more like an order than a question.

“We are responding to a distress call made by the humans on this space station. Would you like me to send you the data on our findings, Captain Calimbaer?” Donaldson asked.
