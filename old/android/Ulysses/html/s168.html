Meanwhile, aboard the Ulysses...

"I wonder what the Sunriser is doing out here, at Pegasi Station," Captain Donaldson mused.

The Cassian military cruiser had come to a halt a thousand spatials from the Ulysses, and simply sat there in space doing nothing. There had been no communications from the starship, and for some reason, that made the captain feel very uneasy.

The feline Cassians were one of the six major species that made the Federation of Worlds. So, they were allies of humanity, and the starship Ulysses. Yet, the standoff position, the silence - it was an aggressive posture, not one of an ally lending their aid. It simply made no sense...
