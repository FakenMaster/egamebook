Suddenly they heard a sound over their wristcoms, it was the proximity alarm sounding on the Bridge of the Ulysses.

"Hang on, Commander," the captain said, and then spoke to someone on the Bridge. "Lieutenant Lewis, what seems to be the problem?"

"Sir, long range sensors have picked up an incoming starship," the voice of the Ulysses' helmsman called out.

"Can you identify it?" the captain asked.

There was a brief pause, then the response, "Sir, it's the Sunriser - a Cassian navy cruiser!"

"A military starship?" the captain sounded surprised. "What's it doing out here?"

The captain addressed Carlson again, "Commander, the Cassians may have called one of their own ships to aid Pegasi Station. I'm unsure as to why a military vessel and not a medical ship or multi-role vessel would show up here. I want you to find out how this cancelled Earth drug got inside everyone, and report to me as soon as possible. We may have a new situation developing here. Ulysses out."

"You heard the captain - let's get to work, people!" Carlson said to his companions.

“If we go back to the Control Centre, I can run scans on the water systems, and see if the drug was put into the water supply,” Gregari suggested.
