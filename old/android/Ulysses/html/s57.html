The three starships slowed to a halt, and the Sunriser began to move out of range while they began charging up all of their available power into their targeting systems and weapons.

Then, in a concerted effort, multiple streams of artificial lightning lashed out from their ships, arcing through the nebula cloud towards the Sunriser...

Only two of the energy bolts hit their target, but without its energy shields, the Sunriser took a harsh beating. Hull plating blasted away into space, glowing red from the superheated energy that bored into the ship.

Captain Donaldson leaned back into his seat, the tension of the moment beginning to fade, as he saw the Sunriser tilt to one side, and begin to drift aimlessly.

“We've got her!” Lieutenant Lewis shouted, to a brief cheer from the other officers.

“Steady now,” Captain Donaldson said. “Our task is not yet completed. We need prisoners – High Command has questions they want answered.”
