“Ulysses to fleet: Don't fire until I give the command, we can't chance them getting away!” the captain ordered.

“Five thousand spatials,” Ensign Hob counted off the distance to their prey.

“Four-point-five thousand spatials,” he continued as minutes slowly ticked by, and the three ships drew closer to the enemy.

“Hold us steady, Lieutenant Lewis,” the captain said to the helmsman.

Suddenly an energy stream lashed out from one of the other cruisers, blasting through the nebula cloud at the Sunriser, but at that range, it missed completely!
