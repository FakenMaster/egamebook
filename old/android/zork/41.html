Leaving the hut behind them, Bivotar and Juranda wade the shallow stream and cross the cavern. At the far end of the cavern, a dark opening leads to another passage.

They enter this new tunnel. The ground here is made of soft sand, slowing their progress.

After walking a while, they come to a point where the passage widens to form a small round room. In the center of the room, painted right onto the sandy floor, is a red "X".

" 'X' marks the spot," says Bivotar with a grin.

"What do you mean?"

"Try digging there with the shovel."

"Come on, Biv. What would that accomplish?"

"Who knows? It won't hurt to try."

"It'll just be a waste of time."

Try digging where the "X" is?

Forget the "X" and continue down the passage?
