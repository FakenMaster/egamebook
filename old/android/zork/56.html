"Well, okay," says Juranda. "I guess it wouldn't hurt to make a few more diamonds..."

Bivotar dashes out to the car and returns with an armful of coal. While Juranda operates the compressor, Bivotar goes to get another armful. Soon they have produced a small pile of diamonds.

Unfortunately, the fine print on the label, which was too faded for Juranda or Bivotar to read, was a warning message. The warning explained that using a Frobozz Magic Compressor more than three times in one day can cause the machine's delicate magic circuits to overload. Sure enough, in the middle of producing a diamond, the compressor explodes. The resulting pyrotechnic display removes Bivotar, Juranda, and a good chunk of the coal mine.
