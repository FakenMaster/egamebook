Juranda and Bivotar continue to crawl along the small tunnel with the slimy floor and the strange rock formations. The air is warm and moist.

Suddenly the floor begins vibrating strangely, and the walls of the tunnel begin contracting around them.

"What's going on?" shouts Juranda, confused.

"I don't know, but let's get out of here," yells Bivotar, crawling back toward the fork. "The amulet has started to glow!"

"Omigosh! Biv, we're not in a tunnel! And those aren't stalactites-they're teeth!"

"Burp!" says the cave behemoth, a short time later.
